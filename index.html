STEEL VS OTHER BUILDING MATERIALS

When looking at cost per square foot, steel will be comparable to wood or other conventional building materials for buildings larger than about 1,200-1,500 square feet. But even if you find that your steel building is a larger initial investment, over the long run your structure can be significantly less expensive to own and maintain.
